How can we help you?

Here are some frequently asked questions from our users.

?
How does WanderGym work?

WanderGym provides you with a single subscription that grants you access to a network of gyms across Ireland. Simply sign up, choose your plan, and start enjoying workouts in different locations without the hassle of multiple memberships.

?
Are there any restrictions on how often I can visit a gym?

No restrictions at all! With WanderGym, you have the freedom to visit any gym in our network as often as you like. Whether you're a daily gym-goer or prefer a more flexible schedule, it's all up to you.

?
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Absolutely. We understand that plans can change. You can cancel your WanderGym subscription at any time with no hidden fees. We believe in keeping things simple and flexible for our users.

?
How do I find gyms in the WanderGym network?

Once you're a member, simply use our user-friendly website to locate gyms in the WanderGym network. You'll find details about each gym, including facilities, opening hours, and user reviews, making it easy to choose the perfect workout spot.

?
Can I share my WanderGym subscription with a friend or family member?

Currently, WanderGym subscriptions are intended for individual use. Sharing subscriptions is not permitted. Each member should have their own subscription for a seamless experience and to ensure access to our diverse network of gyms. If you have friends or family interested, encourage them to sign up for their own WanderGym membership!
